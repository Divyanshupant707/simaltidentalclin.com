Welcome to Simalti Dental Clinic

Located at Mehuwala Mafi, Shimla Bypass, Gali near Chaihan, Simalti Dentals is Dehradun’s premier destination for advanced dental care.

Established in 2020, Dr. Simalti and his team have been dedicated to providing painless and effective treatments in a hygienic environment. We specialize in root canals, cosmetic dentistry, and dental implants.
